Our Services

Manicure

Treat your hands to a professional manicure designed to keep your nails healthy, neat, and beautifully polished. Whether you prefer a natural look or elegant nail art, we create styles tailored to your personality and lifestyle.

Pedicure

Relax and unwind with our spa pedicure treatments. From nail shaping and cuticle care to exfoliation and soothing massage, our pedicures leave your feet feeling soft, refreshed, and perfectly pampered.

BIAB Nails

Our BIAB (Builder in a Bottle) services are perfect for strengthening natural nails while maintaining a beautiful, long-lasting finish. Ideal for clients looking to grow healthier nails with added durability and elegance.

Nail Extensions

Enhance your look with customised nail extensions tailored to your preferred length and shape. From classic styles to modern designs, we create flawless extensions that are both stylish and comfortable to wear.

Gel Nails

Enjoy long-lasting shine and chip-resistant colour with our premium gel nail services. With a wide range of colours and finishes available, gel nails are perfect for achieving a polished and elegant look that lasts

Japanese Head Spa

Experience deep relaxation with our luxurious Japanese Head Spa treatments. Combining scalp cleansing, nourishing treatments, and therapeutic scalp massage, our Japanese Head Spa services help promote scalp health, healthy hair growth, and total relaxation for both mind and body.
